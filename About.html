What is IoLent

IoLent is a digital earning and engagement platform where users can participate in various activities and earn reward points. These points can be used to activate digital robots that generate earnings through the platform's business ecosystem.

Our platform focuses on creating value through user participation, task completion, and community building rather than financial investment or guaranteed returns.

⚙️

How IoLent Works

1

Earn Points

Complete tasks, participate in activities, and invite friends to earn reward points.

2

Point Conversion

100 points can be converted to 1 BDT within the platform's reward system.

3

Activate Robots

1000 points activates 1 digital robot that participates in the platform's earning activities.

4

Generate Earnings

Activated robots generate earnings over time through the platform's business operations.

5

Collect Rewards

Users can periodically collect their generated earnings from the platform.

Business Model

IoLent generates revenue through multiple real business activities such as digital services, advertising, partnerships, and platform operations. User rewards are distributed from these business profits, ensuring a sustainable ecosystem that benefits from actual business growth rather than user investments.

Our model focuses on creating value through legitimate business operations and user engagement, maintaining transparency and fairness for all participants.
