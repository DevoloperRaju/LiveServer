Introduction

At IoLent, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. Referral Data: We track referral relationships and multi-level commission structures to manage our referral system.

Account Management: To create, maintain, and secure your account on our platform.
Service Provision: To provide access to our robot activation system, earnings calculation, and all platform features.

Referral Tracking: To track and manage multi-level referral commissions and relationships.

System Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services and user experience.

Data Security
